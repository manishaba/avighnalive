About Avighna Customize

Steel fabrication processes can often produce waste and scraps when using non-customised materials. This can be costly and disruptive to automation while managing scrap disposal can introduce production cost variations and operational complexities. To solve these challenges, Avighna sources standard quality materials from top global manufacturers like Nippon Steel, Posco, and ArcelorMittal, as well as trusted domestic producers like Tata Steel, JSW Steel, AMNS, and SAIL. We use state-of-the-art processing techniques and equipment to customise precise sizes to our customers' requirements. With extensive inventories from renowned manufacturers, Avighna has become the go-to sourcing partner for many manufacturers, prioritising quality and process optimisation. By eliminating waste, reducing interruptions, and ensuring seamless production, we allow our partners to focus on delivering high-quality products efficiently and cost-effectively.
