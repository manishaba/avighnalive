About Color Coated

Coil coating involves applying decorative and protective organic coatings to pre-painted steel substrates. The sheets may have either galvanized or galvalume coatings. Coated steel sheets find applications in diverse industries and offer numerous benefits, including reduced costs, improved quality, shorter processing times, and energy conservation. They play a crucial role in the construction sector, serving as materials for exterior, interior, and structural components.
