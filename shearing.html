About Shearing

Shearing is a crucial fabricating process involving precisely cutting sheet metal from larger rolls or flat stock. This technique transforms the material into shorter and more manageable lengths by using two blades to pass each other and meticulously divide the material into smaller pieces.

Avighna specialises in shearing operations and can accommodate various thicknesses and widths based on our customers' specific requirements. Our extensive capabilities cover carbon steel, aluminium, and stainless steel flat-rolled materials. We encourage you to connect with our representatives to explore our comprehensive processing capabilities and diverse product lines. You can be assured that we will tailor our services to meet your unique needs and preferences.
