Slitting

Shearing is a fabricating process of cutting sheet metal to size out of a larger roll or flat stock.  The process allows for the material to be cut into shorter lengths to move through the shear machine, cutting two blades being forced past each other in order to fracture the material into separate, smaller pieces accurately and precisely
At Avighna , We can shear various thickness and widths in select locations depending on customer needs. We have the capability to shear carbon steel , Aluminium and stainless steel  flat rolled materials Please contact our representative to learn more about our processing capabilities and product lines
