About Tin Mill Black Plate

Tin Mill Black Plate (TMBP) steel is mainly manufactured from MR Grade Hot Rolled Coils. The Hot Rolling Process generates surface oxide on HR Coils, which are then pickled to remove it. The HR Coils are then cold-reduced to the desired thickness through a Cold Rolling Mill. TMBP is a low-ductility product that usually undergoes further processing to achieve desired end-use characteristics. The product is annealed, temper rolled, and tension-leveled to achieve formability, surface texture, and flatness, respectively, as per requirements. TMBP is primarily used for manufacturing general line cans, automotive and construction components that require malleability. TMBP is highly suitable for producing Tinplate and tin-free steel due to its excellent flatness and uniform thickness, achieved through either the single-reduced or double-reduced process.
