About Avighna Customize

In Most of Steel fabrication processes , there are lot of waste and scraps generating due to use of non customized materials . It leads to not only big cost disadvantages but also interruptions in automations . Further the scrap disposal requires separate and additional process management bringing variations in production costs besides operational difficulties.

Avighna procures the Standard Quality Materials from Leading Manufacturers world wide like Nippon Steel, Posco , Arcelor Mittal and others along with domestic manufacturers like Tata Steel , JSW Steel , AMNS , SAIL and others .With latest Processing techniques and equipments , Company is providing exact size customization as per exact requirements of customers. With availability of large inventories from reputed manufacturers , Avighna has been best sourcing partner for numerous manufacturers for whom quality with process optimization matters most.
