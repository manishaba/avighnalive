About Secondary Steel Processing

The Processing of Secondaries i.e Surplus , Over Rolled , Line Rejections , Quality Defects and Scraps generating from various steel manufacturing processes , has been Primary focus area of the Company and after processing 50-80% of Standard Quality Prime Material has been segregated to re-use as Prime Raw Materials.
These materials are being used as commercial use by MSMEs and SMEs in large Qtys , thereby bringing them significant reduction in raw materials costing. On the other hand , Steel Manufacturers also get the commercial pricing for their scraps and secondaries instead of melting pricings.
This re-processing saves not only environmental pollution caused during manufacturing the steel but brings Win-Win situation for everyone.
