About Us

Based at New Delhi and Mumbai , M/s Avighna Steel Pvt. Ltd. is engaged in the Customization , Processing and Distribution of Carbon Flat Rolled materials mainly Cold Rolled and Coated Products like Galvanised , Pre-painted , Tin Mill Products like Tinplate, Tin free, Black Plate etc for Prime Standard and Secondary Materials . Company has its own sheet processing facilities at A-88 , Taloja , Navi Mumbai ( Maharashtra) from where it supplies customized material sizes to the user industries.

Promoted by Young and Rich Experienced Entrepreneurs Sh. Abhinav Singla and Sh. Ramesh Dass through their sustained and consistence efforts , the Company has established itself as leading distributors of Flat Steel Products especially Tin Mill Products and Coated Steel segment in India. The company is procuring the Standard and Secondary materials through Imports , from Domestic manufacturers/ Importers like Tata Steel, JSW Steel , AMNS , Sail and catering to raw materials requirement of mainly SME/ MSME steel fabricators including manufacturers of Cans and Containers.

Main Business Activities

AvighnaGreen
Secondary Steel Processing

Company is leading processors of Secondary Steel generating from the International and Domestic Steel Mills . Through expert domain knowledge and unique processing services , Company has been able to segregate upto 60-90% of the prime and usable raw materials out of secondary materials. Thus not only it saves valuable environmental costs of manufacturing steel , but commercially win win situation for all stakeholders . The main beneficiaries are SME and MSMEs who use the processed secondary steel as their raw materials requirements which brings them substantial cost advantage of 5-25% to their production cost over the similar prime materials manufactured. The Processed Secondary Steel are in great demand therefore the company is augmenting the processing capacities continuously to cater to growing demand of supply chains across steel industry. Avighna MetalPack
Tin Mill Products

Company has identified “Steel Packaging Raw Materials also known as Tin Mill Products Processing and Distribution” as Focus Area. Approx. 35% of edible products in India wasted due to perish in nature and in absence of preservation in suitable packaging medium , therefore there is immense requirements of hygienic and safe packaging medium where the main ingredients of foods could be stored for longer periods with out change in characteristics and nature of food items . The Tinplate packaging is best packaging medium where being inert properties of Tin Metal , the ingredients of foods do not react with Tin Metal and preservation can be ensured for longer periods . The demand of Tin Plates is witnessing healthy growth rate in India. We aim to provide readymade and customized raw materials to the manufacture / fabricator of Cans and Container Industry for further used for packaging of mainly Edible foods items like Edible Oils , Fruit Pulps , Confectionary , Sea foods and other packaged foods.
