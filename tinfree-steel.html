Tin Free Steel

Tin-free steel (TFS) is a remarkable material that undergoes an electrolytic process to deposit a thin layer of chromium and chromium oxide onto a cold-rolled sheet steel base, resulting in a stunning metallic finish on both sides. TFS stands out in various aspects, including exceptional paint adhesion, superior heat resistance, outstanding abrasion resistance, resistance to black sulphide stains, aesthetic appeal, high workability after lacquering, impressive corrosion resistance, solderability, and weldability. This combination of attributes makes TFS a versatile and high-performance choice for applications where durability, aesthetics, and functionality are essential, setting it apart as a top-quality material in metallurgy.
